Privacy Policy

At KarNit Rapid Sourcing, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us through our website karnitsourcing.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when filling out our forms, including:

  • Your name, company name, and email address
  • Phone number and delivery location
  • Details about your quote request or product inquiry

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for business communication and service delivery, including:

  • Preparing and sending quotations
  • Providing sourcing and procurement services
  • Improving our website and communication process

3. Data Protection

All data submitted through our contact or quote request forms is handled by a thirdparty service provider. Although, we do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any other third parties except as required to fulfill your sourcing request or comply with legal obligations the data is accessible to our third party service provider and is protected by thier Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies do not store personally identifiable information and can be managed through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Formspree to process form submissions. Data sent via our contact or quote forms is handled according to their Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us directly.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised update date.
